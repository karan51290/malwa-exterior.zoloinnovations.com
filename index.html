Featured Projects

Roofing & Siding Work

In this subarban home, we did the roofing and siding renovations. We installed Hardie fiber cement siding for durability and aesthetics, complemented by asphalt shingles for long-lasting weather protection. Our meticulous approach ensured proper insulation and ventilation, enhancing both appearance and structural integrity. The result? A stunning transformation that not only enhanced the visual appeal but will also ensure the home's longevity and resilience.

Vertical Siding & Smart Board

In this project, we undertook the construction of a contemporary commercial building, focusing on vertical siding and Smart Board installations to achieve a sleek and modern aesthetic. We utilized vertical siding to create clean lines and a distinctive architectural profile. Smart Board, known for its durability and versatility, was meticulously installed to enhance both the facade's visual appeal and structural integrity.

Smart Board, Vinyl Shakes and Siding

In this charming residential property, Smart Board, vinyl shakes, and siding were expertly integrated to create a visually captivating exterior. Smart Board, renowned for its durability and versatility, formed the foundation of the facade, providing a sturdy and modern backdrop. To add depth and character, vinyl shakes were strategically incorporated, lending a timeless appeal. Complementing these elements, vinyl siding was meticulously installed to ensure a seamless and weather-resistant finish
